## Overview

Keep documentation structured for all your projects.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Project">
    Go to your dashboard and click "New Project".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation Pages">
    Click "Add Page" to create sections like Setup, Usage, or FAQ.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Hit "Publish" to make docs visible to your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
