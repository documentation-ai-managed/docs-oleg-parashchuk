## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An email address for account verification
- Access to `https://dashboard.example.com` (the main dashboard URL)

<Callout kind="info">
  No credit card or payment information is required to get started. You receive a free tier with unlimited documentation spaces.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click the <kbd>Sign Up</kbd> button. Choose email registration or sign in with Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link inside.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="edit-3">
    Enter your name and preferred workspace name, then submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication during setup for added security.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, create a space to organize your projects.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="home">
    From the dashboard, select <kbd>New Space</kbd>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit">
    Enter a name like "My Project Docs" and add a brief description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Choose visibility (public or private) and set a brand color like `#3B82F6`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Space" icon="rocket">
    Click <kbd>Create Space</kbd> to open your new documentation area.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Document

Spaces support MDX documents for rich, interactive content.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Quick Editor" icon="edit">
    Click <kbd>New Document</kbd> in the sidebar. Use the visual editor to add headings, lists, and components.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="MDX Code" icon="code">
    Select <kbd>New MDX File</kbd>. Paste code directly into the editor.

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: My First Doc
    description: Hello world example
    ---

    ## Welcome

    This is your first document using `{`title`}` frontmatter.
    ````

    ````mdx
    <Callout kind="success">
      Document saved automatically!
    </Callout>
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
